Helpful details to include.

STATION 1

Where is the issue?

Describe the room, fixture, or appliance that's causing trouble. Is it the kitchen sink, basement drain, or upstairs bathroom? The more specific you are, the faster we can diagnose the problem and bring the right tools.

STATION 2

What changed recently?

Did the problem start after a storm, a renovation, or new appliance installation? Any recent changes help us trace the root cause. Even small details like unusual noises or slow drains can point us in the right direction.

STATION 3

Is water actively running?

If water is flowing right now, tell us immediately. Know where your main shut-off valve is located. If you can safely turn it off, do so. This information helps us prioritize your call and give you emergency guidance over the phone.
