Our Residential Plumbing Services

Fast, reliable solutions for every pipe, drain, and fixture in your home. Our expert services cover everything from emergency leaks to full bathroom renovations.

Leaks and Pipe Repairs

From dripping faucets to burst pipes, we diagnose and fix leaks fast. Our team uses advanced leak detection to find hidden water damage before it spreads, saving you money on costly repairs.

Clogged Drains

Slow sinks, backed-up showers, or foul odors? We clear blockages in kitchen, bathroom, and main sewer lines using hydro-jetting and professional augers that get the job done right the first time.

Toilet and Faucet Repairs

Running toilets, wobbly handles, or low water pressure? We repair and replace all major brands of faucets and toilets, restoring comfort and efficiency to the most-used fixtures in your home.

Water Heater Support

No hot water in the morning? We install, repair, and maintain traditional tank and tankless water heaters so you always have reliable hot water when you need it most.

Kitchen and Bath Plumbing

Planning a renovation or dealing with an outdated setup? We handle sink installations, garbage disposals, shower valves, and full fixture upgrades with precision and care.

Maintenance for Properties

Keep your plumbing in top shape with regular inspections and preventative maintenance. We serve homeowners and property managers across New York, helping you avoid emergencies before they start.

How a service visit flows.

01

Tell us what is happening

Reach out by phone or our quick online form. Describe the issue — a drip, a clog, or an emergency — and we'll listen carefully so we arrive prepared with the right tools and parts for your residential plumbing services.

02

Our team reviews your situation and confirms a convenient appointment window. We'll explain what to expect, provide a transparent estimate, and answer any questions before we even head your way.

A licensed Plumb Me technician arrives on time, inspects the problem thoroughly, and walks you through the findings. Using advanced diagnostic tools, we pinpoint the root cause before any work begins.
